Past Projects


IARC Mission 7

The International Aerial Robotics Competition or IARC called for building a drone that is capable of interacting with moving ground robots.

Investigation Into Motion Control for Robotics

Research project to learn about motion control algorithms, with the goal of creating a motion control library that can be used on a variety of robots.

Scorpion

Creating a scorpion robot (six-legged) that can mimic the motion of a real scorpion.

Mini Sumo

Small autonomous driving robot where the goal is to push the opposing robot outside of the ring.

Micromouse

Another small autonomous driving robot that has to solve a large floor maze as fast as possible.

Sailbot

Building autonomous sailbots that will face various tests including speed, navigation, obstacle avoidance and more.

Artbot

Create a robot panther (Pitt's mascot) that is both functional and aesthetically pleasing.

Line Follower

Line following robot where the goal is to complete the track as fast as possible.

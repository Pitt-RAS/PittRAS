Intro to ROS

Robot operating system, or more commonly known as ROS, is a 100% open source set of software libraries and tools that assist with the creation of advanced and sophisticated robot applications. With drivers to state-of-the-art algorithms, it creates a standard for the robotics industry and helps users create applications rapidly. We use ROS and lots of our projects and it's an amazing tool to have under your belt before going out to industry!

How to Join

Whether you are just starting out or you have years of experience, you can find a place to contribute at RAS. The best way to get involved is to join a project team. You are free to join a project at any point and to move between teams should you find one that suits you more. In addition, you can find out more information about current and past projects, including current project meeting times, on the Projects Page. We love meeting new people, so get in touch by joining our mailing list and Discord* below!

*In November 2020, we have phased out our slack channel in favor of Discord. You might see the slack URL floating around still so please note we will likely not respond there. Join our Discord!
